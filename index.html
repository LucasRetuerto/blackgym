Welcome to BlackGym

In our gym you will find a friendly atmosphere, with qualified teachers to help you achieve your goals. It does not matter if it is your first time in a gym or if you are already an expert.

We have modern equipment, and a large number of machines to make your training as dynamic as possible.

We are waiting for you at BlackGym, a place where, in addition to training, you will surely make great friends.

Our Programs

We have different programs to offer you, you can combine them or you can select the one that best suits your objective.

CROSSFIT

CrossFit is a system of physical conditioning that is based on exercises that vary constantly, with functional movements, executed at high intensity.

FUNCTIONAL

Functional training is based on performing exercises that adapt to the natural movements of the human body to work muscles and joints globally.

FREE PASS

You will have free and unlimited access to the gym machines at the indicated times. Ideal if you don't have much time and want to train alone.
